# 4Geeks

[4Geeks](https://4geeks.com) is an **AI residency**. You come in. You rebuild yourself. You leave AI-first and ready to win in the world.

Award-winning programs and top-tier partnerships back the residency; graduates and companies keep coming back with strong testimonials.
Trusted by leading employers and institutions worldwide.

- **You come:** From any background—you don’t need to already be technical. You show up with the intention to change how you work and think.

- **You rebuild:** The residency is built to strip away old habits and assumptions. You learn AI and machine learning, LLMs, prompt engineering, and how to ship AI in real products—with AI guiding and accelerating your learning every step of the way.

- **You become AI-first:** By the end, AI isn’t a tool you use sometimes; it’s how you approach problems, build, and lead. You think, build, and communicate with an AI-first mindset.

- **You go back:** You don’t stay forever. You return to your company, your industry, or your next venture—with a new skillset, a new way of working, and the confidence to succeed in an AI world.

If you’re ready to step in, rebuild, and go back as someone who wins with AI—[4geeks.com](https://4geeks.com) is the residency for that.
